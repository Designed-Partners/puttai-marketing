Features

Your caddy in your pocket

Built by a golfer, for golfers. PuttAI combines real physics with your phone's sensors to give you the edge on every green.

📐

Measure Slope Automatically

Place your phone flat on the green, tap Measure Slope, and the built-in gyroscope detects the exact grade.

🎯

Precise Aim Points

Enter distance, slope, elevation, and stimp. PuttAI calculates how many cups to play and shows where to start your line.

🔀

Multi-Break Putts

Reading a double-breaker? Add multiple segments with different slopes. PuttAI combines them into one clear aim point.

👟

Stride Calibration

Set your personal stride length so distance calculations are accurate for you. Walk it off in steps, PuttAI converts to feet.

💪

Pace Guidance

Not sure how hard to hit it? PuttAI factors in elevation and gives you pace advice — firm for uphill, soft for downhill.

📴

Works Offline

No internet required. PuttAI works anywhere on the course with a dark, glare-free interface built for outdoor use.

How It Works

Four steps to better putting

Get your aim point before every putt in just seconds.

1

Pace the Putt

Walk from ball to hole counting your steps. PuttAI converts to feet using your calibrated stride.

2

Measure Slope

Place your phone on the green and tap Measure. The gyroscope reads the exact grade automatically.

3

Set the Stimp

Enter the green speed. PuttAI adjusts break calculations so faster greens show more movement.

4

Get Your Line

See exactly how many cups to play with a visual aim point and pace recommendation. Commit and putt.
